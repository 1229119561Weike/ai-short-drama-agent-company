TikTok distribution follows the same final-action gate as [[public-platform-final-action-requires-confirmation]]: stage the caption and asset, report the ready-to-post state, and wait for explicit user confirmation before clicking the public Post button.

Do not mark TikTok distribution complete from a staged upload or browser-ready state. Settlement requires the TikTok URL, post ID, public/Everyone status, caption used, published asset path, and confirmation that the local release CSV or equivalent distribution record now includes the TikTok publication URL.
