`storage/files/short-drama-research/` is the v0.1 storage contract for durable short-drama research assets, with daily summaries, individual signals, hook banks, narrative formats, local-culture notes, case studies, do-not-copy risk notes, and taxonomies.
Each signal should carry at minimum schema version, signal id, created date, platform, region, evidence type, verification status, genre, core conflict, hook pattern, comment divide, conversion mechanism, copy risk, and recommended creative use. This lets later intake or meta-system workflows consume research safely without treating unverified observations as facts. [[research-source-boundaries]]
